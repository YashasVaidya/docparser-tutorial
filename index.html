Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Can Docparser handle scanned PDFs?

A1: Docparser primarily works with digital PDFs. For scanned documents, OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is required.

Q2: How do I integrate Docparser with other applications?

A2: Docparser offers API integrations with various platforms like Zapier, making it easy to connect with other applications.
